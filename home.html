Fizzyo Project - Complete Case Study

The Fizzyo Project is my second year project I was involved in, along other teams from my home university (UCL). We worked closely with the NHS and Microsoft to create a solution to poor treatement adhesion in children that suffer from Cystics Fibrosis. It was a great project that helped us improve on our communication, teamwork and time management skills.

This article documents the progress and overview of how our project evolved throughout the academic year, with close attention to the start of it: the design process.

Sworks Prototypes and research

Sworks is my CS3240 team's project. It is a platform that aims to create a in-campus freelancing community, as there are many students who are in constant need of people to help them with tasks they do not have the skills for, and getting professional help is quite pricy for university students.
This article comments on some of the prototyping, user study and design choices done throughout the process of coming up with our final interactive prototype.
